# Replication Instructions

This document explains how to reproduce the empirical results for the project:

**"News Language Use Around U.S.-Involved Geopolitical Events"**

The repository collects New York Times articles surrounding several major geopolitical events, extracts article text, constructs readability and lexical features, and estimates event-based pre/post regressions.

---
